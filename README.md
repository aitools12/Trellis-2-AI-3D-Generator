## What is Trellis 2?

Trellis 2 is a web-based AI generation site for creators who want to turn prompts or references into usable visual assets. It supports practical AI art workflows for campaign visuals, product scenes, social posts, concept art, and fast creative iteration.

## Why use Trellis 2?

Trellis 2 gives creators a direct browser workflow for image generation and art direction. Users can describe a visual idea, generate options, compare results, and keep refining until the image is ready for a draft, presentation, listing, or campaign.

## How Trellis 2 works

1. Open Trellis 2 and start with a prompt or reference idea.
2. Choose the generation workflow that matches the visual result you need.
3. Review, refine, and export the generated image asset.
